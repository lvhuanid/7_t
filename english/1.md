## essential

1. essential for / to … (对…必不可少)
   例：Water is essential for life.

2. it is essential (that)… (…是极其重要的)
   例：It is essential that you arrive on time.

3. essential difference / nature (本质区别 / 本质)

✅ 我给你两版“标准答案”（都正确，难度不同）
版本一（最贴近你原意，简洁自然）：

To improve your English speaking, daily practice is more important than memorizing a dictionary, and this is essential.

版本二（用 it is essential that 开头，更书面）：

It is essential that you practice every day, because daily practice matters more than memorizing a dictionary for improving your spoken English.

版本三（高级一点，用 essential for 作状语）：

For improving your spoken English, daily practice is essential, and it's far more useful than just reciting word lists.

## significant

- a significant difference (显著差异)

- significant improvement (显著进步)

- it is significant that… (…很有意义/很重要)

✅ 标准答案（三档难度，任君挑选）
🔥 基础稳妥版（最接近你的原意）：

My English listening improved significantly last year, and this is significant for finding a job.

🚀 进阶流畅版（用现在完成时 + 避免重复 significant）：

My English listening has improved significantly over the past year, which is crucial for my job hunting.

💎 装X精简版（用名词作主语）：

The significant improvement in my English listening over the past year matters a lot for my career.

## available

- available to + 人 (对…可用/对…有空)

- available for + 事情 (可用于…/有空做…)

- readily available (随时可得的，唾手可得的)

✅ 标准答案（你离满分只差这一小步）
版本一（最贴近你原意，稳妥修改）：

I'm available tomorrow afternoon. If you need help, you can come to me, and the new practice materials are available for download.

版本二（更口语化、自然一点）：

I'm available tomorrow afternoon. Feel free to come to me if you need help. Also, the latest practice materials are now available for download online.

🎁 给你一个“救命规律”（专治时间介词）
有 this / next / last / tomorrow / yesterday → 不加任何介词
✅ tomorrow morning / next week / last night

只有早/午/晚或星期几 → 加 in 或 on
✅ in the morning / on Monday

## adapt

- adapt to + 环境/变化 (适应...)

- adapt yourself to ... (让自己适应...)

- adapt from ... (由...改编而来)

To adapt to the new work schedule, I need to adapt my daily routine.

✅ 标准答案（给你三版，直接拿去背）
🔥 保守修正版（最贴近你的原句）：

I spent more time adapting to the culture here when I came to the new company. Luckily, I quickly adapted with the help of my colleagues.

🚀 自然口语版（更流畅，推荐优先记这个）：

When I first joined the new company, I spent a lot of time adapting to the culture. Luckily, my colleagues helped me adapt quickly.

💎 高级精简版（用上 adapt myself，强调主观能动性）：

It took me a while to adapt myself to the culture when I joined the new company, but luckily, my colleagues helped me get through it.

🎁 一个价值连城的“小规律”（专治 spend）
记住这个万能公式，一辈子不会错：

人 + spend + 时间/金钱 + (in) doing something

✅ I spent 2 hours **finishing** my homework.

❌ ~~I spent 2 hours **to finish** my homework.~~

（把这条刻进DNA，四级写作至少涨5分）

📢 关于 college vs colleague 的友情提醒
college /ˈkɑː.lɪdʒ/ = 大学（名词）

colleague /ˈkɑː.liːɡ/ = 同事（名词）

发音和意思都不同，考场上一旦写混，考官会愣住。今天开始，把 colleague 记进你的“职场词库”里！

## diverse

- diverse range —— 广泛多样的范围
- culturally diverse —— 文化多元的
- diverse opinions —— 不同的意见

我给你改写成自然又地道的版本（四级水平）：

A successful team needs diverse skills and backgrounds.

或者更高级一点：

A successful team needs people with diverse skills and backgrounds.

## challenge

- face/take on a challenge —— 面对/接受挑战
- challenge someone to (do) something —— 挑战某人做某事
- a big challenge for (someone) —— 对某人来说是个大挑战

Public speaking is a big challenge for me.
